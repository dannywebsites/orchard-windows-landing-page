This cookie policy explains what cookies are, how Orchard Windows Limited ("Orchard," "we," "us") uses them on our website, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to site owners.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

2.3 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to:

  • Measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising
  • Understand which ads led you to our website
  • Track conversion events (e.g., when you submit a home survey request)
  • Build audiences for future advertising (e.g., people who visited our site but did not request a survey)

The Meta Pixel may collect data including your IP address, browser information, page views, and button clicks. This data is sent to Meta Platforms, Inc. and may be used in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy.
